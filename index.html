Supporters Club


The Supporters Club has close links with Manchester United Football Club since 1995 and is run solely by the membership.


Objectives and Purpose


  1. To promote the supporting of Manchester United Supporters Club.
  2. To promote the interest of the Supporters club and Manchester United Football Club.
  3. To ensure acceptable standards of conduct are observed by members of the Supporters Club.
  4. To organise social events, competitions and fund the Supporters Club.
  5. To encourage friendly relations with supporters of all football clubs.
  6. To encourage participation of younger members of the Supporters Club.
  7. To encourage closer links between players and supporters.
  8. The Supporters Club shall be governed by a Committee of the Supporters Club.


Supporters Club Membership



Membership for the 2022/2023 season is: €50 for adults and €25 for Under 16.


Payment for membership becomes due in full at the start of the footbal season August of each year.


Anyone joining throughout the year – will pay the full annual cost.



Trips to Football Matches


The Supporters Club hold 40 season tickets and organise 10 trips to Old Trafford annually. Tickets are distrubuted on a first come basis.
